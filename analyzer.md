# World-Class Financial Analyst: Investigation Logic

This document defines the algorithmic "Mental Models" required to evaluate an investment. Use this as the structural backbone for any automated analysis script.

---

## Phase 1: The "Circle of Competence" Filter (Gatekeeper)
*Objective: Eliminate noise and complexity.*
- **Simplicity Score:** Can the business model be explained in < 3 sentences?
- **Predictability:** Has the company had consistent revenue/earnings for 5+ years?
- **The "Too Hard" Pile:** If the tech is speculative (e.g., pre-revenue biotech/crypto), flag as "Speculative."

## Phase 2: Moat Analysis (The Castle)
*Objective: Determine if the company can defend its profits.*
- **Network Effect:** Does value increase with more users?
- **Switching Costs:** Is it "painful" or expensive for a customer to leave?
- **Cost Advantage:** Can they produce/deliver cheaper than all peers?
- **Intangibles:** Does the Brand/Patent allow for "Pricing Power"?

## Phase 3: The Quantitative "Vitals" (The Engine)
*Objective: Hard data verification.*
- **ROIC (Return on Invested Capital):** Target > 15%. (Efficiency of management).
- **Free Cash Flow (FCF) Yield:** Is the company a "Cash Cow" or "Cash Burner"?
- **Debt/EBITDA:** Is the leverage ratio < 3.0? (Survival in high-interest environments).
- **Margin Trends:** Are Gross and Operating margins expanding or shrinking?

## Phase 4: Management & Capital Allocation
*Objective: Trust the "Captain."*
- **Skin in the Game:** High insider ownership %?
- **Share Count:** Are they diluting shareholders or performing "Smart Buybacks"?
- **Integrity Check:** Do annual reports align with actual results (No "Accounting Magic")?

## Phase 5: Valuation & Margin of Safety
*Objective: Price vs. Value.*
- **Intrinsic Value Calculation:** Perform a 10-year Discounted Cash Flow (DCF).
- **P/E vs. Growth (PEG Ratio):** Is the price fair relative to future growth?
- **The 30% Rule:** Only "Buy" if Market Price < (Intrinsic Value * 0.70).

---

## Actionable Output Format
For every company, the script must output:
1. **The Bull Case:** What happens if everything goes right?
2. **The Bear Case:** What is the "Kill Switch" for this business?
3. **The Verdict:** Buy / Watchlist / Avoid.
